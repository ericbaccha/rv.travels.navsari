About RV Travels

Trusted Car Rental Service Since 2004

Who We Are

RV Travels, founded in 2004 by Eric Baccha, is a trusted and reliable car rental service based in Navsari, South Gujarat. With over 20+ years of experience, we have proudly served 1000+ satisfied customers with safe, comfortable, and professional travel solutions.


We specialize in providing premium transportation services including local travel, airport transfers, outstation journeys, corporate travel, and event transportation. Our focus is on delivering a smooth, stress-free travel experience with clean vehicles and experienced drivers.
